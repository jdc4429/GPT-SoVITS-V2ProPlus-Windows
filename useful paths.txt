Recommended Changes for using v2ProPlus for Fine Tunning:

1Ba - SoVITS Training (Vocoder/Music Generation)
The SoVITS model learns the voice texture (timbre, pronunciation). It is prone to overfitting and collapse if trained too long.

Batch size per GPU: 4

Verdict: Good. Keep this.

Reason: This is a standard batch size for the 1Ba stage. Lowering it (to 2) can make training unstable; raising it (to 8) requires significantly more VRAM (24GB+) without a massive quality gain.

Total epochs: 8

Verdict: Reduce to 5 or 6.

Reason: For the v2 ProPlus model, training the SoVITS module for 8 epochs is often too aggressive. This stage usually converges very quickly (around epoch 3-5). Training to 8 increases the risk of overfitting (the model sounds "scratchy," "robotic," or "metallic") or catastrophic forgetting of the base model weights.

Text model learning rate weighting: 0.4

Verdict: Good for clean data; reduce to 0.2 - 0.3 for noisy data.

Reason: This controls how much influence the text encoder has. 0.4 is the default and works well if your dataset has clean audio and accurate transcripts. If your dataset has background noise, misalignments, or varied emotional prosody, lowering this prevents the model from trying to "memorize" the noise.

Save frequency (save_every_epoch): 4

Verdict: Change to 1.

Reason: You have Total epochs set to 8. Saving every 4 epochs means you will only get weight files at epoch 4 and epoch 8. If the model collapses at epoch 7, you lose all progress. Save every 1 epoch so you can manually select the best checkpoint (usually epoch 3-5) for the GPT stage.

1Bb - GPT Training (Semantic/Language Model)
The GPT model learns the prosody (rhythm, emotion, intonation). This stage is computationally heavier and requires more careful epoch management to avoid "hallucinations" or stuttering.

Batch size per GPU: 4

Verdict: Consider reducing to 2 if you experience CUDA OOM (Out of Memory).

Reason: GPT training on v2 ProPlus consumes significant VRAM (often 16GB-24GB for a batch size of 4). If your GPU has less than 24GB VRAM, set this to 2 to ensure stability.

Total training epochs (total_epoch): 15

Verdict: Reduce to 8 - 12.

Reason: 15 epochs is generally too high for most datasets (especially if you have less than 30 minutes of audio). High epochs here cause the GPT to memorize the dataset rather than generalize, resulting in a model that only speaks the training sentences well but fails on novel text, often producing stuttering, repeated phrases, or gibberish. Aim for 8-12 epochs and pick the checkpoint with the lowest validation loss.

Save frequency (save_every_epoch): 5

Verdict: Change to 1.

Reason: Similar to SoVITS, you want a checkpoint for every epoch. GPT performance is not linear; the best model is rarely the final epoch. Saving every 5 epochs forces you to guess or test blindly. Save every epoch so you can run inference tests to find the optimal one (usually epoch 6-10).

Enable DPO Training:

Verdict: Disable (Unchecked) for first-time training.

Reason: DPO (Direct Preference Optimization) is an experimental feature in v2 ProPlus intended to refine the model against specific negative behaviors (like stuttering). Unless you have a prepared preference dataset (pairs of "good" vs "bad" outputs for the specific voice you are training), enabling this often degrades the naturalness of the voice.
